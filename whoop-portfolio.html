The Research

The case study that
started the conversation.

I identified a massive underserved segment hiding in WHOOP's existing user base — members whose unique physiology makes current algorithms inaccurate.

🧠

ADHD Members

🔬

PCOS Members

Hormonal fluctuations and insulin resistance (present in 50-70% of PCOS women) affect strain tolerance and recovery — but WHOOP's recommendations stay static.
