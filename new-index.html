The Research

The case study that started
the conversation.

I identified a massive underserved segment hiding in WHOOP's existing user base. Members whose unique physiology makes current algorithms inaccurate.

ADHD Members

Different autonomic nervous system regulation leads to HRV patterns that WHOOP misinterprets. What the app labels "poor recovery" may actually be their optimal state.

PCOS Members

Hormonal fluctuations and insulin resistance (present in 50 to 70% of PCOS women) affect strain tolerance and recovery, but WHOOP's recommendations stay static.
